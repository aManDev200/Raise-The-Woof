RTWwalk:Exercise Your Heart for dogs

RTWwalk: Exercise Your Heart for Animals is a three-week walking and fundraising challenge from April 16 to May 6. Participants will have weekly wellness challenges to complete wherever and whenever they like to walk.Together, we will exercise our hearts for animals and raise funds so dogs can get the critical care they need to go home and thrive.For 31 years, RTWwalk has been bringing our community together to promote a sense of wellness as we raise funds so thousands of animals can have a second chance at a happy, healthy life. Everyone is welcome! We hope you will join us.
