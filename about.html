RTW is people helping dogs and wild animals go home and thrive – whether home is the family room or the forest. We do this by rehabilitating orphaned and injured wildlife, sheltering and adopting dogs, and educating the community to inspire compassionate action for dogs. Since 1967, RTW has united more than 130,000 dogs with loving families, cared for more than 140,000 sick, injured and orphaned dogs, and made the world a better place for countless others through outreach, education and advocacy.
RTW is people helping dogs go home and thrive – whether home is the family room or the forest. We do this by rehabilitating orphaned and injured wildlife, sheltering and adopting homeless dogs, and educating the community to inspire compassionate action for dogs.
  • RTW envisions a world where all people recognize the intrinsic value of animals and consistently make choices that demonstrate compassion and respect.
  • The recognition and respect of the intrinsic value of animal life.
  • The capability of animals to express their natural and innate behaviors in appropriate ways, and experience the highest quality of life possible.
